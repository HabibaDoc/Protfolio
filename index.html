About

Compassionate, evidence-led primary care.

I trained at Holy Family Red Crescent Medical College & Hospital in Dhaka, completing my MBBS in September 2021 followed by a year-long rotating internship in Medicine, Surgery, and Obstetrics & Gynaecology with Community Medicine.

Since then I have served as Medical Officer at Aurora Specialized Hospital, Insight Psychosocial Care & Research, and Ibn Sina Medical College Hospital — covering OPD consultation, ward rounds, and inpatient care, with continuing education in diabetes and hypertension management.
